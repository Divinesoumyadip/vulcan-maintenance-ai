# OEM Manual (Extract) — Continuous Caster 2 Mold Oscillator, Unit CC2-MO-01
Revision: D (2025-08-12)  |  SYNTHETIC demo document for hackathon use

## Section 4.2 — Vibration Limits (drive-side housing, RMS velocity)
- Normal operating band: <= 4.5 mm/s
- Alert / warning limit: 7.0 mm/s — schedule inspection within 7 days
- Trip / critical limit: 11.0 mm/s — stop oscillation, do not restart
  without bearing inspection

## Section 4.3 — Bearing Temperature
- Normal: <= 70 degC at drive-side housing
- Warning: 75 degC; Trip: 90 degC

## Section 6.1 — Drive Bearing Specification (category)
Drive-side and idle-side bearings are spherical roller type rated for
oscillating duty and mold-level shock loads. Replace as a matched pair.
Order against the OEM catalog using the unit serial plate — do not
substitute generic catalog equivalents without OEM concurrence.

## Section 6.4 — Replacement Duration
Planned drive-side bearing pair replacement, including alignment and
oscillation stroke verification: 5 to 6 hours with a trained crew of 3,
crane support required.

## Section 7 — Restart Verification After Bearing Work
1. Oscillation stroke and frequency within +/-2% of setpoint.
2. RMS vibration <= 4.5 mm/s after 30 min run-in.
3. Bearing temperature stabilized <= 70 degC within 2 h.
