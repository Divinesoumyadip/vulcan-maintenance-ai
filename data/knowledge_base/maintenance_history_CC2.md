# Maintenance History (Extract) — CC2-MO-01 Mold Oscillator
SYNTHETIC demo records for hackathon use

## WO-23-1187 (2023-09-14) — CONFIRMED FAILURE
Symptom: rising drive-side vibration over ~2 weeks, 3.1 -> 8.4 mm/s.
Found: outer-race spalling, drive-side spherical roller bearing.
Root cause (post-mortem): lubricant degradation from cooling-water
ingress past a worn labyrinth seal.
Action: bearing pair + labyrinth seal replaced; relubrication interval
halved. Post-repair vibration: 2.6 mm/s.
Outcome: no recurrence for 14 months.

## WO-24-0412 (2024-11-03)
Oscillation irregularity traced to servo valve contamination (hydraulic),
NOT bearing-related. Vibration signature was broadband, not harmonic.
Valve flushed and filter upgraded.

## WO-26-0156 (2026-04-19) and WO-26-0233 (2026-05-14)
Brief oscillation irregularity events, self-cleared; no teardown done.
Noted for trend watch.
