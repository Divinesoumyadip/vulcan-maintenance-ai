# SOP-VM-012 — Vibration Monitoring & Response, Caster Area
Revision: 3 (2025-11-02)  |  SYNTHETIC demo document for hackathon use

## 3. Response Matrix
- Above warning limit: raise work order within 24 h; increase reading
  frequency to every 4 h; notify shift engineer.
- Above 80% of trip limit: shift supervisor approval required for
  continued operation; plan stoppage at next available window.
- At/above trip limit: stop equipment. LOTO before any inspection.

## 5. Permits & Isolation
All work on the oscillator table requires: LOTO of oscillator drive,
hydraulic isolation of the oscillation circuit, and a caster-area
permit-to-work. Mold area is a hot-work-adjacent zone — confirm strand
status with the caster control room before entry.
